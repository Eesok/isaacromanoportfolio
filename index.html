About Me

Click my Image!

I am a creative software engineer with a proven knowledge of programming, web design, and full stack application development. I always focus on the growth and positivity of myself and those around me. My biggest strength is the ability to adapt and change.
When I graduated high school, I went to Israel to serve as a Paratrooper in the Israel Defense Forces. At first I used this as an opportunity to learn more about myself, life, and where I fit in society. After serving for 2 years, I had a grand realization that I never want to define myself as a specific type of person. I want to live a life of change, growth and inspiration.
Being that my natural talents and interests were in science and mathematics, I went to college and majored in Mechanical Engineering. This experience was both exhilarating and challenging. All of the math equations that I came across throughout this part of my life helped develop my mind and shape me to become more oriented towards computer science.
While studying full time in college, I was also working full time as a sales rep for an online jewelry website. Being that I served in the military in a foreign land, I was up for the challenge. The communication skills that were developed from the military experience made my time as a sales rep fun and successful. It goes without saying that my mental disciplinary muscles were in pretty good shape so juggling these two full time commitments we doable, although quite challenging, as things ought to be to enable real growth.
After delving into advanced mathematics and physics courses, I stumbled across the course “Intro to Computer Science”. This changed everything for me. I fell in love with the art of coding. I did tremendous research and decided to switch gears out of college and towards an immersive software engineering boot-camp.
Now I know how to code in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Python, React, Django, JQuery, Node, Express, MySQL, PostgreSQL, and MongoDB.
